## Using Templates

1. Click the **Templates** button in the ribbon toolbar (Tools group)
2. Browse templates by category or search
3. Click a template to preview it
4. Click **Use Template** to apply it

The template code is loaded into the editor and rendered in the preview pane. You can then modify it with follow-up prompts or edit it directly in the Monaco editor.

## Saving Custom Templates

After generating code you want to reuse:

1. Open **App Settings** for your generation
2. Give it a name, icon, and category
3. The app is saved and accessible from the history sidebar

Custom templates persist in IndexedDB and are available across browser sessions.
